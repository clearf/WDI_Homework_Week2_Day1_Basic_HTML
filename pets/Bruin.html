Bruin

"That dog's helped us win a lot of games. He's a true warrior. Been down since day one"

- Kobe Bryant

Bruin is a 10 year old daschund, and Kobe's most loyal companion. With Bruin at his side at 2002, Kobe was able to achieve a new kind of spiritual clarity to which he attributes his super-start play during thata season.
