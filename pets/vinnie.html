A Crazy Cat Named Vinnie

  • Age: 5
  • Gender: Female
  • Favorite toys:
    • Mouse on a string
    • Anything outside in nature

Vinnie was found at 5 weeks old underneath an office building in Asbury Park, NJ and adopted by our client Irene Mottola. From her first months of life, Vinnie was a firecracker, constantly running around and snapping at the resident humans. These days, she prowls the grounds of her home both day and night looking for a fight. But she always saunters back to the house with a triumphant gait...
