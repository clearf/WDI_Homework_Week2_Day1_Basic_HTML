  • Scout is a 3 year old Boston Terrier.

Kobe Bryant has contributed almost as much to the game of basketball as he has to animals and the broader movement for animals' rights awareness. In his eighteen seasons as a Laker, Kobe has organized numerous events on behalf of the organization "Pets for Vets", which pairs shelter animals in the hands of recovering war veterans. Together we can make a difference.
